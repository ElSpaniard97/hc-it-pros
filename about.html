About Us

HC IT Pros is a family-owned IT company with over 10 years of hands-on experience supporting small and mid-sized businesses across the Texas Hill Country. We combine proven expertise in networking, cybersecurity, and systems management with the power of AI-driven automation to streamline processes, reduce downtime, and improve efficiency for our clients.

Our mission is simple — to deliver enterprise-quality technology solutions with the personal touch and reliability only a local, family-run business can provide.

Founded in the heart of the Texas Hill Country, we take pride in helping our community thrive through dependable IT partnerships. From proactive support and security monitoring to advanced AI integration, our goal is to make technology a seamless part of your success.
