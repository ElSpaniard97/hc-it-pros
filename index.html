Your Local IT Partner

Family-owned and Hill Country-based, HC IT Pros provides professional IT support and cybersecurity for small businesses. We blend modern solutions with personal service to keep your operations secure, efficient, and connected.

Our Mission

To empower local businesses across the Texas Hill Country with reliable, affordable, and innovative technology solutions—bridging the gap between enterprise-grade IT and community-driven service.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity – We do what’s right, even when no one is watching.
  • Reliability – Your business uptime is our top priority.
  • Innovation – We stay ahead of emerging technologies to keep you protected.
  • Community – We believe in strengthening local businesses that form the backbone of our community.
  • Personal Service – Every client is treated as a priority, not just a ticket number.

Our Goals

  1. Simplify Technology – Make IT approachable and easy to manage for small business owners.
  2. Enhance Security – Protect Hill Country companies from modern cyber threats through proactive defense and monitoring.
  3. Promote Growth – Use technology to help clients operate efficiently and expand with confidence.
  4. Build Relationships – Establish long-term partnerships rooted in trust, transparency, and quality service.
